Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Scope

This website is an official landing page for our Roblox game and community pages only. We do not operate as a large platform — this site provides game information, links to Roblox, and community details.

What we collect

We collect only minimal information necessary for site functionality and analytics:

  • Non-personal technical data: IP (for server), browser, device type, timestamps (for logs and basic analytics)
  • Data you voluntarily provide: messages via contact form or email (only if you send them)

We do not sell personal data. We do not store payment information (Roblox payments are handled entirely by Roblox).

Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to monitor site performance and usage. These tools collect aggregated, non-identifying metrics — active players, visits, and pageviews. You can opt out by disabling cookies in your browser.

Third-party services

We link to Roblox and may use external services for hosting, analytics, and CDN (GitHub Pages, Cloudflare, Google). Each third party has its own privacy policy.

Children

The content is primarily about a Roblox game. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Data retention & security

Contact messages may be kept until the issue is resolved. We protect data with standard measures provided by our hosting and Cloudflare. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed, but we follow common best practices.

Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version is available at /privacy. Significant changes will be noted with an updated date above.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact us via the contact page or email listed there.
